INTRODUCTION :

Welcome to Inquisitors AI and Quiz Society, a student-driven community dedicated to innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. Our mission is to empower students with practical knowledge in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Web Development, Project Management, and emerging technologies through workshops, internships, real-world projects, and collaborative teamwork. We believe that learning goes beyond classrooms. By working on industry-inspired projects and participating in technical events, our members gain valuable skills that prepare them for successful careers in technology.

OUR MISSION :

To inspire students to become future technology leaders by providing quality learning opportunities, hands-on experience, mentorship, and a collaborative environment where innovation thrives.

OUR VISION :

To build a community of passionate learners and innovators who create impactful AI-powered solutions for real-world challenges while promoting teamwork, creativity, and lifelong learning.

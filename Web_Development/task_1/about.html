WHO WE ARE :

Inquisitors AI and Quiz Society is an educational society that brings together students who are passionate about technology, artificial intelligence, and innovation. We provide a platform where students can learn, explore, and grow by participating in practical projects, technical workshops, competitions, and mentorship programs. Our society encourages curiosity, leadership, teamwork, and continuous improvement while preparing students for the rapidly evolving world of technology.
